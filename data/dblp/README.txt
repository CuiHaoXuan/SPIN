


dblp dataset is the extracted from DBLP database by:
    1.  use only inproceedings articles
    2.  articles that appear after 2000
    3.  there is a link between pair of authors if they coauthor more than 10 or 15 papers
    4.  extract limited number of nodes from the network

dblp.inp.2000.10.100 is the dataset with inproceedings article after 2000, copaper over 10, 100 node in the article

use LDA to get topics

statistics.txt     is the statistics of different datasets
